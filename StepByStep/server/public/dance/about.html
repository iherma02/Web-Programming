About Us

Step-by-Step was created by a team of Tufts students made up of dancers and non-dancers alike who wanted to really shake up the way people learn to dance. Inspired by our wide range of dance experiences, we made learning choreography with Step-by-Step as accessible as possible–not limited to an intimidating dance class or a fast-paced, hard-to-follow YouTube tutorial. Our site allows users to search through YouTube and efficiently and easily learn new moves from any video. We hope that through Step-by-Step, an aspiring dancer or hopeful learner will gain the experience of a professional dance class from the comfort of their own home.
