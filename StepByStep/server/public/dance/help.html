Our software is simple and intuitve

  • How do I use Step by Step to learn new dance moves?

    • Search for the song, artist, or dance style you want to learn from and simply click on the video that speaks to you! Then, you can click “Save Current Time” to generate buttons for the timestamps of the moves you want to learn. A list of clickable timestamps will show up for you to click through when you’re ready to learn and rehearse the steps.

  • What can I do in the video viewer?

    • Watch a YouTube video and use the “Save Current Time” button to save and rewatch the parts of a dance you’d like to learn. Click through any of your timestamps to jump to that part of the video!

  • How do I make an account?

    • Navigate to “Account” (in the sidebar) and choose the “Sign Up” option!
